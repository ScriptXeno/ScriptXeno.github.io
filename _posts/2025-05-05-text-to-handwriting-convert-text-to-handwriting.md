## **What is Text-to-Handwriting?**

**Text-to-Handwriting** is a web-based tool that takes plain text and turns it into a realistic handwritten image or downloadable PDF. Created by **Nakshatra Ranjan Saha**, the idea came from a genuine need to automate the boring, manual process of writing school assignments. The tool is available [on GitHub](https://github.com/OCEANOFANYTHING/text-to-handwriting) and [its official site](https://oceanofanything.github.io/text-to-handwriting/).

## **Key Features of Text-to-Handwriting**

- Converts any text to handwriting-style images
- Offers image output and **PDF generation**
- Customizations for font, paper, ink, margins, spacing, and more

## **Handwriting Fonts and Custom Font Uploads**

This tool supports over **38+ handwriting fonts**, giving you a diverse range to choose from. But what makes it truly personal is the ability to **upload your own handwriting** as a font.

### **How to Upload Your Own Handwriting**

You can generate your personal font using tools like [Calligraphr](https://www.calligraphr.com/):

1. Sign up on Calligraphr (Free account is enough)
2. Create a minimal template and download it
3. Print and fill the template with your handwriting
4. Scan and upload it back to Calligraphr
5. Download your `.ttf` font file
6. Upload it in Text-to-Handwriting via the "Upload your handwriting font" button

Easy as that! Now, your digital handwriting assignments are *literally* written in your style.

## **Tech Stack Behind the Tool**

This isn’t just a design gimmick—it’s built on a solid foundation:

## **FAQs**

### 1. **How can I add my own handwriting?**

Use Calligraphr to create a handwriting font and upload the `.ttf` file in the tool.

### 2. **Why are there gaps between letters in custom fonts?**

Use the letter spacing and word spacing sliders to fix the layout.

### 3. **Can I upload my own paper background?**

Yes! Use the “Use Image As Paper” button to upload your custom paper texture.

### 4. **Will there be more updates?**

Nope. This is a **read-only project** now. You’re free to fork it and build your own version. Maybe in the future, they'll add more features, but for now, it’s a one-time project.

### 5. **What happens if the tool doesn't work on my system?**

Make sure your browser supports PWA and JavaScript. Try switching browsers or clearing cache.

### 6. **Is the output image editable after generation?**

No, but you can regenerate with changes.

### 7. **Does it work on iPads and tablets?**

Yes, it’s fully responsive and works across devices.

### 8. **Can I use this for printing greeting cards or letters?**

Absolutely! Just choose your paper theme and font style accordingly.

### 9. **Can I remove the watermark?**

There is **no watermark** in the generated output.

### 10. **Is it safe to upload custom fonts or images?**

Yes, everything is processed locally in the browser. No data is stored or sent to servers.

### 11. **Can I use this tool for commercial purposes?**

Yes, but please check the licensing of any custom fonts or images you upload.

### 12. **Where can I find the source code?**

You can find the source code on [GitHub](https://github.com/OCEANOFANYTHING/text-to-handwriting). Feel free to fork it and make your own version!
